Artist Statement

Hi, my name is Mandy. I am third year graphic designer student at San Jose State University. This is the portfolio of my career as a graphic designer. In here you can find my previous work that I did in my digital media class or on my own terms.

In my spare time, you can find me at the gym, at the kitchen cooking or sitting in front of a laptop for hours to make art happen. I enjoy doing digital art, because it makes me feel excited when I finish a project. I love creating art, from videos editing to making logos or web designing. I love seeing my progress in digital art as I build myself as an artist. You can get in touch with me through the contact page if you discover my arts interest you.
